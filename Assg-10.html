About Special Characters

In HTML, you can use special character entities to display symbols that have a reserved meaning in HTML.

Here are some examples of special characters:

  • Ampersand: `&` → &
  • Less Than: `<` → <
  • Greater Than: `>` → >
  • Copyright Symbol: `©` → ©
  • Registered Trademark: `®` → ®
  • Trademark Symbol: `™` → ™
