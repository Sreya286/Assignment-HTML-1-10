My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Drishyam
    • Year Released: 2013
    • Main Actors: Mohanlal, Meena, Ansiba Hassan
    • Description: A gripping thriller about Georgekutty, a cable TV operator, who goes to great lengths to protect his family when they get entangled in a police investigation. The film's brilliant screenplay keeps viewers guessing till the end.
  2. Kumbalangi Nights
    • Year Released: 2019
    • Main Actors: Fahadh Faasil, Shane Nigam, Soubin Shahir
    • Description: Set in the picturesque fishing village of Kumbalangi, this heartwarming drama revolves around four brothers and their dysfunctional relationships. It beautifully portrays themes of love, brotherhood, and mental health, with a standout performance by Fahadh Faasil.
  3. Premam
    • Year Released: 2015
    • Main Actors: Nivin Pauly, Sai Pallavi, Madonna Sebastian
    • Description: A coming-of-age romantic drama that follows the life of George and his encounters with love at different stages of life. The film is beloved for its refreshing storyline, vibrant characters, and excellent music.
  4. Vikram
    • Year Released: 2022
    • Main Actors: Kamal Haasan, Vijay Sethupathi, Fahadh Faasil
    • Description: A high-octane action thriller centered on a covert agent's mission to track down a group of masked vigilantes. The movie was noted for its strong performances and intense action sequences.
  5. Bangalore Days
    • Year Released: 2014
    • Main Actors: Dulquer Salmaan, Nivin Pauly, Nazriya Nazim
    • Description:A feel-good drama about three cousins who move to Bangalore and navigate through the challenges of life, love, and friendship in the vibrant city.
